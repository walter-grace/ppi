## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific**: Include brand, model, or reference numbers when possible
2. **Price Ranges**: Specify price ranges for better results (e.g., "under $10,000")
3. **Categories**: Mention if you want watches, trading cards, etc.
4. **Analysis**: Ask for analysis or arbitrage opportunities explicitly
5. **Combined Queries**: Ask the chatbot to search and then analyze
